FOOD SPECIALITY OF KENYAN PEOPLE

Ugali
A dish made of maize flour, millet flour or sorghum flour cooked in boiling water
Matoke
A green banana, often steamed in its own leaves and mashed
Chapati
A thin pan cake of unleavened whole meal bread cooked on a giddle
